Engineering Services

AKSA offers engineering services for design, development, prototyping, and manufacturing to our valuable customers as per industrial and military standards at a competitive price.

AKSA also extends specialist HR services to its clients for performing operation, maintenance, and life cycle support at their premises while having distant administrative control. The specialties include electronics, electrical, mechanical, and HVAC services. Our customized services include scheduled, periodic, preventive, and corrective maintenance according to OEM’s laid down standards, procedures, manuals, codes, regulations, and SOPs, especially for the avionics and aviation industry.
