AKSA Generator Remote Monitoring Gateway

AKSA Generator Remote Monitoring Gateway (AGRMG)

We provide one of the finest solution (AGRMG) for remotely monitoring critical parameters of Diesel Generator for the ease of operations and quick recovery in case of malfunctioning. AGRMG with a built in self-test capability is cost effective Distant Observer System which facilitates direct access to various sensing devices, monitors health, detects and troubleshoots, along with provision of software and firmware updates over the Internet.

Specifications

Sensor Interface: Wired Interface: Digital, Analog, RS-232, RS-485

Communication Interfaces: Datalink Channel: 3G and Ethernet Protocol: TCP/IP

Input Power: 8V ~ 30V, 1A

Wireless data acquisition capability
