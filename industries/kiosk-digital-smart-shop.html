KIOSK - Digital Smart Shop

KIOSK - Digital Smart Shop

Digital Smart Shop is designed and developed for financial and telecom Services to provide 24/7 services to the customers. Digital Smart Shop can be placed at different places like airports, malls, hospitals, cinemas etc.

It is also used to disperse prepaid/postpaid SIMs to the customer after a certain amount of money has been put into the machine.

Key features of the system are:

Purchasing of prepaid/postpaid SIMs

Money transfer

Bill payment

User Management

Mobile Account Registration

VAS/Bundles

Language Selection option

Cash Deposit

CDRs/Tax Certificate

Screen layout

Cash Withdrawal

IHT

PIN Reset
